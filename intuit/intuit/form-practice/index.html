Having fun with forms

The outlines that surround the various sections of this form are declared by the 'fieldset' tag. Within the opening fieldset tag, you may declare a boolean attribute 'hidden,' which will hide the entire fieldset. You can do the same for another boolean attribute, 'disabled,' which will not hide the section by disable it. If hidden is already in the same opening tag, the hidden will be nulled and overriden by the disabled. Another boolean, which can be placed in almost all tags within a form is the 'required' attribute, which will prevent a form from being submitted unless the portion of the form that is required is filled out - no form can be submitted while a required portion is left unfilled. Both name and email form controls include boolean attributes required and placeholder.
